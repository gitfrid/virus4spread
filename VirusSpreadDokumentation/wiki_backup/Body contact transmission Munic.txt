## **Body contact transmission Munic**
<br>
<br>If the virus itself does not move, as in the example, then it must be transmitted between moving people over very short distances, 
<br>e.g. through physical contact. 
<br>
<br>This curve rises much more slowly than with airborne transmission, it reaches a constant horizontal value and does not oscillate. 
<br>
