## **Lockdown example Munic-Airborne**
<br>
If the movement of people is almost completely restricted, the oscillation is interrupted and the curve drops sharply, 
<br>but as soon as movement resumes, it rises sharply in return.
<br>

**What is amazing is that it seems as if the system remembers the intervention, as it now continues to oscillate with two peaks.**
<br>
